Welcome to History Club!

BLS History Club is the premier history club at BLS, and regularly hosts exciting events, with regular meetings and competitions. Our club is a great resume builder, and not to mention super fun and rewarding! We compete in the National History Bowl & Bee, as Academic Worldquest, earning Champion titles and qualifying in Nationals in both!
